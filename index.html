Core Features

Everything You Need to Buy & Sell Precious Metals

Purpose-built tools for gold buyers, coin dealers, and precious metals shops. No workarounds, no compromises.

Live Spot Prices

Gold, silver, platinum, and palladium prices pulled from Kitco every 30 seconds. Always know the market.

Smart Calculator

Select karat, enter weight, set your percentage. Melt value and offer price calculated instantly from live spot.

Customer Management

Full customer profiles with ID capture, transaction history, and lifetime value tracking. Know your buyers and sellers.

Inventory Tracking

Track every item by metal, karat, weight, and location. See hold status, cost basis, and profit/loss at a glance.

Compliance & Holds

Automatic hold periods for every buy. Track active holds, overdue items, and flagged transactions in one dashboard.

Refinery Tracking

Create shipments, assign inventory, and track status from sent to settled. Know exactly what's at the refinery.

Multi-Location

Manage multiple shops from one account. Each location has its own inventory, employees, and reports.

Data Export

Export transactions, customers, and inventory to CSV. Import data via our bulk tools. Your data, your way.

How It Works

Three Steps. That's It.

From customer walk-in to completed transaction in under a minute.

01

Add the Customer

Look up an existing customer or create a new one. Capture their ID photo and info for compliance.

Calculate the Offer
